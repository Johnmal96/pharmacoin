BIG PHARMA FACTS

Price Gouging

In the U.S., insulin costs 5-10x more than in other developed countries. The same vial that costs $300+ in America costs just $30 in Canada.

Patent Manipulation

Big pharma companies extend patents through "evergreening" - making minor modifications to existing drugs to maintain monopolies and prevent affordable generics.

Executive Compensation

In 2023, the average Big Pharma CEO made over $22 million while patients struggle to afford life-saving medications.

Marketing vs. Research

Major pharmaceutical companies spend 2-3x more on marketing than on research and development of new treatments.

Medical Bankruptcy

Nearly 60% of all bankruptcies in the U.S. are related to medical expenses, with prescription drug costs being a major contributor.

Global Access

One-third of the world's population lacks access to essential medicines, while pharmaceutical companies maintain profit margins of 15-30%.
